Online Premium Payment

Online payment is supported by versions IE 9+ | Chrome 35+ | Mozilla 35+ | Opera 30+ | Safari 5+


  • For any query regarding policy payment you can write us at service.helpdesk@axismaxlife.com
  • For payments made post 3:00 PM IST, the premium receipt will be available on My Account on the next working day.
  • An advance premium can be paid within the same financial year for any premium due in that financial year. For any premium payment due in the next financial year, the same can be paid three months prior to the premium due date.
  • Mandatory: Please update your PAN/Form 60* (if PAN is unavailable) for your policy with us. Update PAN via My Account / service.helpdesk@axismaxlife.com
  • *Form 60 accepted at service.helpdesk@axismaxlife.com / Axis Max Life branch. Ignore if already submitted.
  • If any excess premium is received by Axis Max Life then the same shall be refunded on the same account / credit card subject to availability of respective account details for NEFT except for policies on monthly or quarterly frequency.
  • Please note: Axis Max Life shall not be liable to pay any interest on the said amount.
